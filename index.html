About Me

I was born on September 16, 1992, in California. However, not longer after I was born, my family moved to Singapore, where I have spent the majority of my life. Now, I'm back in the US for college, going to school at Washington University in St. Louis, on track to receive a Bachelors in Business Administration. Being born in California but growing up in Singapore, and now going to school in the US makes me feel lost; they call us "Third Culture Kids" (google it!). I plan on staying in the US after I graduate, so I welcome any and all job opportunities that are located in the US! Even though I'm majoring in Finance and Marketing, I'm not entirely sure what exactly I want to do post-graduation. I will have some tough decisions ahead as I get closer to graduation.
