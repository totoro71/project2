The Alpha Phi Chapter of Alpha Phi Omega was chartered at Washington University in St. Louis on May 1, 1936. The chapter rechartered October 10, 1981.

How to Rush

What is a Pledge?

Before you become a brother of Alpha Phi Omega, all members are required to compelte a pledging process during their first semester. The process is open to all years during the beginning of each semester. We are non-selective! In order to pledge, you must attend 1 info session, 1 rush event, and complete an intent-to-pledge form.

Rush Requirements

APO has a 9-week Pledge Process. The week average is ~5 hours.

Principles

  • 15 hours of Service
  • 7 hours of Fellowship
  • Complete the Leadership requirement
Events

  • Attend all rituals: Pledge Ritual, Big Brother Reveal, and Initiation
  • Attend Pledge Retreat
  • Participate in Pledge Fundraising, Service, and Fellowship Events
Meetings

  • Attend all pledge meetings
  • Attend 2 chapter meetings
  • Attend 1 active exec meeting
  • Attend 1 pledge exec meeting
APO Knowledge

  • Pass pledge quizzes and pledge final
  • Obtain all pledge signatures
  • Obtain 15 active member signatures
  • Dues ($35 at Pledge Ritual and $35 at Initiation
Key Dates for Spring Semester

  • Pledge Meetings every Thursday from 7-8:30pm
  • February 14: Pledge Ritual
  • February 27: Pledge Retreat
  • March 8: Pledge Service
  • March 9: Reflections
  • March 16: Initiation
